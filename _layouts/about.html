About Hmovie

Hmovie is a digital catalog of over 400+ Hmong movies- aiming to preserve the history of Hmong movies, film-making, and storytelling.

In a digital era where technology eases the consumption of media, Hmong movies donâ€™t have that sort of luxury. Their stories are often trapped in VHS tapes or completely forgotten by Hmong generations.

Hmovie challenges the extinction of Hmong cinematography by bringing Hmong movies to the forefront using modern-day technology.

About the Site

Hmovie was officially launched in May 2025. The site was developed under a fellowship program hosted by the Ramsey County Historical Society and Center for Hmong Studies. This project was pursued by fellow, Linda Lor- an upcoming software developer who aspires to bridge technological gaps in her local community.

The site is hosted with GitHub pages and utilizes the web framework Jekyll to create a dynamic, yet static, site.
